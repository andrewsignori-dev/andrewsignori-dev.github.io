About Me

My name is Andrea Signori, and I am from Italy. I’m a Data Scientist and Analyst.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Economics and Finance at the University of Brescia in Italy in 2019, and I obtained my Master's degree in Data Science within a Double Degree program with Trier University (Germany) in 2022.

I developed my master thesis through a joint research collaboration with Harvard University, studying time-dependent forecasting methodologies across econometrics and machine learning.

In 2025, I completed a PhD in Technology, Innovation, and Management, focusing on Data Science applications in the aviation industry.

Within my role, I aim to unveil the stories hidden in data—transforming raw numbers into insights that guide decisions, uncover trends, and bring clarity to complexity.

I enjoy crafting compelling data narratives, whether through interactive dashboards, visualizations, or analytical reports, turning data into knowledge that empowers action and sparks understanding.
